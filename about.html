Our Story

The Hangar Coffee Company has humble roots as a startup based out of a truck touring Kansas City. Gaining close relationships with many residents and businesses around KC, The Hangar set up roots and opened its doors in 2019 to our first brick and mortar location right in the heart of downtown Kansas City. We are honored and humbled to have found such a welcoming community and have been fortunate to spread to another KC location in Overland Park, a downtown STL location, and soon to spread into Austin, TX! As such we strive to give back to our loving communities and 5% of earnings go right back into nonprofits and organizations local to each location.

Farm-Direct Relationships      

We are committed to offering ethically purchased and responsibly produced products of the highest quality. We pride ourselves on building real relationships with our suppliers to ensure our producers are getting their needs met to produce the highest quality coffee. This means we spend time in coffee growing regions around the world with our coffee farmers to address their real needs. The Hangar commits another 5% of earnings to benefit the coffee producers. This includes everything from building coffee nurseries, tiling fermentation tanks, to investing in their education system!
